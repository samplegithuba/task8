Welcome to Our Company

The Company Ganapathy Industries has established its business on the foundation of efforts, ethics and commitment. It was founded in 1998, with a mission to become an international, organization manufacturing heavy duty hydraulic press machinery. We are renowned manufacturers, suppliers of hydraulic Deep Drawing press, Bakelite moulding press, rubber moulding press, ms part press,baling press manufacturer, hydraulic power pack, hydraulic Cylinder and R&D machine. The company is promoted by a man of vision and ideas, Mr. J.Elumalai possessing extensive experience in the Hydraulic Press Machine Industry.

The success mantra of the company has been to always act quickly to the changing consumers requirements and adopt quick measures to fulfill these needs. The company maintains as well as monitors distribution network with online connectivity and keep itself persistently abreast with all the latest advancements of hydraulic technology and industrial application owing our commitment to words our business.
